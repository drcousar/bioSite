Introduction

Taylor is from Pearl Mississippi and attends Pearl Junior High School. Early in life, Taylor developed multiple ear infections, which led to speech issues. Through years of therapy Taylor went from a speech impairment to no impairment at all. As a matter of fact, Taylor is now a public speaker and speaks very well. Taylor has leveraged her power of public speaking into positions of leadership where she speaks in front of large audiences. Taylor was the Vice President of the student council and also has served as President of the Beta Club.

Taylor is also a star athlete. She is a Striker on the soccer team. Taylor is a crowd pleaser and scores many goals. Taylor is also on the volleyball team. She plays so well, she often gets elected to play with the older kids. Her teammates call her "Tipping Tay", because of her knack for tipping the ball over the net, saving the ball from hitting the floor on their side of the net.

Taylor's goal is to become a lawyer. I am just proud that she is a well rounded kid. I don't know if her awesome grades or sports abilities will get her a scholarship, but even without one, she's the best in my book.

Taylor's Sports

Taylor started her soccer career at the age of 5, in the local city recreation league. At the end of her first season, she was awarded Most Improved player of the season. After a few season, Taylor earned herself a reputation as a Striker. She scores more than most of her opponents and teammates. She and her teams have won 6 championships, most of which at the District and State levels.

She also plays different positions such as back and mid field defender.

She has just finished her first season on the 7th Grade Volleyball team. She has done very well, in fact she was the only 7th grader asked to suit up and play with the 8th graders. She was labeled with a nickname that depicts her signature move "Tipping Tay".

She starts as a setter in volleyball.

She hopes to make the college volleyball and soccer teams and win scholarships.

Taylor's School Accomplishments

Taylor is a straight A student.

She has been elected into the Vice Presidency of the Student Council, and also elected the President of the Beta Club honor society.

She has been accepted to take all honors classes.

Her favorite subject is Mathematics and also Reading. She has recently signed up for band and plays percusion, and is showing promise as a talented drummer!

Taylor hopes to attend an Ivy League school, ultimately earning a degree in Law.

She is also in the process of learning Spanish in her free time.



Taylor's Career Goals

Taylor has her heart set on being an attorney.

She hasn't decided on a what she will specialize in just yet, but her overall goals are specific.

She plans to Graduate High School by the age 18. As mentioned earlier he hopes to attend an ivy league college, earn Bachelor's degree by the age of 22, and finally her Juris Doctorate by the age of 25.

Taylor plans to immediately enter the field and work for a law firm for ten years to gain experience. After gaining ten years experience, whe aspires to open her own law practice.
